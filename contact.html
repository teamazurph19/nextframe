Collaboration FAQ

Everything you need to know about collaborating with NEXTFRAME Philippines.

Do you accept student collaborations?

Absolutely! We support students, young Filipino creators, and beginner portfolio builders. Send us your sample work and a short pitch about your idea.

What is the typical project timeline?

Timeline varies by complexity, but most student-friendly creative collaborations can be planned within 4-8 weeks from initial concept to final output.

Do you provide equipment for shoots?

We can work with available school facilities, campus-friendly setups, and lightweight creator gear depending on the project needs.

Can we license your existing library?

Yes, much of our work is available for educational and commercial licensing. Contact us with the specific "Gallery" link.
